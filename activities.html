Teaching & Supervision

Dr. Ahmed has extensive teaching and supervision experience. As an Assistant Professor at COMSATS University Islamabad (Wah) from 2014 to 2021, he taught undergraduate and postgraduate courses, supervised final‑year projects and MS/PhD theses, and secured grants to establish research laboratories. He also served as convener of the accreditation committee responsible for program accreditation under the Washington Accord. In his current role at Khalifa University he continues to co‑supervise students and contribute to annual teaching activities.

Administrative & Outreach Activities

Beyond teaching and research, Dr. Ahmed actively contributes to institutional administration and outreach. He has served as convener and member of committees responsible for PEC documentation, industrial visits, graduate programs, internal audit and orientation programmes. He has helped launch the IEEE student chapter as the founding counsellor and led initiatives on career counseling, internships and alumni engagement. He also supports tree plantation and clean/green drives, ISO quality management system trainings and accreditation focal roles.
