Research Focus

Advancing wireless communications, UAV monitoring and secure hardware through cutting‑edge research.

Research Interests

Dr. Ahmed’s research spans real‑time monitoring of commercial UAVs using computer vision and remote identification data, design of SPDM‑based zero‑trust chip‑to‑chip authentication protocols, asynchronous non‑orthogonal multiple access (NOMA) with offline packet repair and recovery, and development of reliable wireless and satellite communication systems for Internet of Things applications. His broader interests include hardware security, energy‑efficient network slicing, hybrid automatic repeat request (HARQ) strategies, and integrated terrestrial‑wired and LEO satellite networks for 6G IoT.
